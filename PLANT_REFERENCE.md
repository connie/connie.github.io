## 1. Hydrangea arborescens (Smooth Hydrangea)

| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Deciduous shrub |
| **Height × Width** | 3–5 ft × 4–6 ft |
| **Foliage** | Dark green; turns yellow in fall |
| **Bloom Color** | Greenish-white → snow white; dries to parchment tan |

| **Season of Interest** | Summer (blooms), Fall (dried heads), Winter (exfoliating bark) |
